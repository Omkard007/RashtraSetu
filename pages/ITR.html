Infrastructure Development

Impact of Taxes: 25% of revenue goes to national infrastructure.

Major Projects
  • National Highways – 15,000 km expansion
  • Metro Projects – 10 cities
  • Smart Cities Mission – 100 cities
  • Airport Development – new terminals & runways
  • Ports & logistics hubs to boost trade
Citizen Benefits
  • Faster travel & improved connectivity
  • Enhanced urban living & smart infrastructure
  • Better trade & economic growth

Official portal: infrastructure.gov.in

National Defence

Impact of Taxes: 20% of revenue strengthens the armed forces.

Key Investments
  • Modern fighter jets, tanks & naval ships
  • Border surveillance & security infrastructure
  • Cyber defense & AI technology
  • Disaster management & emergency response
Citizen Benefits
  • Peace of mind & national security
  • Rapid disaster response & relief
  • Global defense credibility

Official portal: mod.gov.in

Healthcare Services

Impact of Taxes: 15% funds public healthcare & schemes.

Programs & Schemes
  • Ayushman Bharat – 1.5 Cr beneficiaries
  • National Health Mission – vaccination & maternal care
  • Public hospitals & clinics
  • Health research & innovations
  • Emergency medical services
Citizen Benefits
  • Affordable/free healthcare access
  • Improved life expectancy & quality of life
  • Preventive & emergency care support

Portal: nhp.gov.in

Education Development

Impact of Taxes: 12% funds education initiatives.

Projects & Programs
  • Government schools, colleges, universities
  • Scholarships & grants for underprivileged
  • Skill development & vocational training
  • Research funding & digital learning
Citizen Benefits
  • Affordable and accessible education
  • Improved employability & skills
  • Support for higher studies & research

Portal: education.gov.in
