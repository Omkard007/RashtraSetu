Public Support Services

Government schemes and assistance for citizens

Healthcare Support

Ayushman Bharat — Medical treatment benefits for eligible families.

Objective

Provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare services to all citizens, especially economically weaker sections, reducing financial burden.

Vision

Ensure a healthier nation with universal health coverage, improved life expectancy, and reduced disease burden.

Mission
  • Implement health insurance & cashless hospitalization schemes.
  • Expand Health & Wellness Centers for preventive care.
  • Provide financial protection against catastrophic health expenditure.
  • Strengthen public healthcare infrastructure nationwide.

Employment Assistance

Skill India — Training programs to enhance job opportunities.

Objective

Empower youth with industry-relevant skills, vocational training, and entrepreneurship opportunities to enhance employability.

Vision

Make India a global leader in skilled workforce and reduce unemployment.

Mission
  • Conduct skill development programs aligned with industry standards.
  • Provide certifications to improve employability.
  • Support entrepreneurship & startup initiatives for youth.
  • Collaborate with industries for employment placement.

Student Scholarship

Government scholarship schemes for higher education students.

Objective

Ensure access to higher education for economically weaker and meritorious students.

Vision

Develop a knowledgeable, skilled, and competitive youth capable of contributing to national development.

Mission
  • Provide merit-based scholarships for secondary & higher education.
  • Support tuition fees and academic expenses.
  • Encourage marginalized students to pursue quality education.
  • Promote inclusive access to education.

Women Support

Empowering and protecting women through government initiatives.

Objective

Promote gender equality, ensure safety, provide economic support, and empower women socially and professionally.

Vision

Create an empowered society where women participate in economic, social, and political life.

Mission
  • Implement skill development, training & employment programs.
  • Provide financial assistance and subsidies for women entrepreneurs.
  • Ensure safety and legal protection nationwide.
  • Promote awareness campaigns for gender equality.

Farmer Support (PM-KISAN)

Financial assistance for farmers to support agriculture.

Objective

Provide direct income support to small & marginal farmers to improve financial stability and promote sustainable agriculture.

Vision

Create a prosperous agricultural sector with modern farming techniques and enhanced income security for farmers.

Mission
  • Directly transfer financial support to farmers’ accounts.
  • Promote modern farming methods & training.
  • Support crop insurance & disaster relief schemes.
  • Encourage sustainable agriculture practices.
